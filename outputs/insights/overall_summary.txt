reveals extreme and unjustified cost variation in the US healthcare system.
The average cost variation ratio across specialties is 1153.9x, meaning
the most expensive provider for a given procedure costs 1153.9 times

## Geographic Disparity
Regional analysis shows a 32.8% cost differential between the most and
least expensive regions. Patients in high-cost areas face substantially higher
